About Spinomenal Slots

Spinomenal is an Israeli game developer that has been creating engaging slot experiences since 2014. They have carved out a strong niche with their Book-style slots, Celtic and Irish themes, and Hold and Win mechanics. With a growing portfolio of over 200 titles, Spinomenal offers South African players a rich selection of games with solid RTPs and diverse themes.

Why SA Players Love Spinomenal

Spinomenal excels at the Book slot genre. Their Book of Demi Gods series (parts II and III) takes the expanding-symbol-in-free-spins mechanic and layers it with mythological themes and progressive features. Book of Irish Treasures and Book of Clovers bring Celtic charm to the format. For players who prefer different mechanics, Majestic King and Majestic Leprechaun offer unique royalty and luck-themed gameplay.
