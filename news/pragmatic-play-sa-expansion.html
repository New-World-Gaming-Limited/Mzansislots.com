Pragmatic Play Slots: Complete Provider Guide

Guide | 22 March 2026 | By MzansiSlots Editorial Team

Pragmatic Play is the most played slot game developer in South Africa. Their titles appear across every major platform, and games like Gates of Olympus and Sweet Bonanza are consistently the most-played slots in the country. This guide covers who they are, what makes their games distinctive, and how to explore their catalogue.

About Pragmatic Play

Pragmatic Play is a content provider for the iGaming industry, founded in 2015 and headquartered in Sliema, Malta. Despite being a relatively young company compared to giants like NetEnt or Microgaming, they have become one of the most prolific and widely distributed slot developers in the world.

The company holds gaming licences and certifications in numerous regulated markets, including the UK Gambling Commission, Malta Gaming Authority, Gibraltar Regulatory Authority, and several others. This broad regulatory coverage enables their games to be legally distributed across dozens of international markets.

Pragmatic Play releases approximately three to five new slot titles every month. This sustained output has given them a portfolio of over 300 slot games, covering a wide range of themes, volatility profiles, and mechanics. They also produce live casino content, virtual sports, and bingo products, but slots remain their most significant business segment.

What Makes Pragmatic Play Slots Distinctive

Several characteristics appear consistently across the Pragmatic Play catalogue and help explain their popularity with South African players:

Tumble mechanics with multipliers. Many of their most popular games use a tumble (cascading) mechanic where winning symbols are replaced by new ones. This is combined with growing multipliers, particularly during free spins rounds. The combination creates explosive win potential and a dynamic playing rhythm that keeps players engaged.

High maximum win potential. Pragmatic Play games consistently advertise high maximum win caps (often 5,000x to 25,000x or more). While these outcomes require rare combinations of events, the potential creates an aspirational quality that appeals to players seeking larger wins.

Bonus buy options. The majority of their high-volatility titles include a feature purchase option (bonus buy), priced at 100x the spin bet. This allows players who want to experience the bonus round directly without base game play to do so. See our bonus buy guide for a detailed explanation of how these work.

High release frequency. The consistent release cadence means licensed SA operators always have new Pragmatic Play titles available shortly after their global launch. Players who enjoy exploring new games will find regular fresh content from this provider.

Mobile optimisation. All Pragmatic Play games are built with HTML5 technology for seamless performance on mobile devices. This is particularly relevant in South Africa, where a large proportion of online gaming occurs via smartphone.

Gates of Olympus: South Africa's Favourite Slot

Gates of Olympus is, by most measures, the most played online slot in South Africa. The game uses a 6x5 tumble grid where symbols cascade down after wins, with multiplier symbols (showing x2, x3, x5, x10, x15, x20, x50, or x100) appearing randomly during spins to multiply the total win at the end of the round.

The bonus round (free spins) features an unlimited growing multiplier that accumulates with each cascade. A high cascade count during the bonus, combined with high-value multiplier symbols landing during play, creates the conditions for very large wins. The game's maximum win is 5,000x the bet.

The Ante Bet option (+25% to base spin cost) doubles the chance of triggering the bonus, giving players control over their risk profile. The full bonus buy at 100x is also available for direct feature access.

Sweet Bonanza: Cluster Pays and Multipliers

Sweet Bonanza uses a candy-themed 6x5 grid with cluster pays (groups of 8 or more matching symbols create a win, regardless of payline position). The free spins round introduces multiplier symbols (x2 to x100) that multiply wins when they appear. Multiple multiplier symbols on the same spin multiply each other, creating the potential for enormous results.

Sweet Bonanza has an RTP of 96.51% (in the standard version), making it one of the higher-RTP Pragmatic Play games available. The bonus buy costs 100x the current spin bet.

The Big Bass Franchise

Big Bass Bonanza launched in 2020 as a fishing-themed, medium-high volatility slot with a distinctive bonus mechanic: during free spins, each fish symbol reveals a cash prize, and fisherman symbols "collect" all visible fish prizes, multiplied by the number of fishermen active. The mechanic is clear, satisfying, and delivers well-defined bonus round outcomes.

The game's success spawned a franchise now spanning over ten titles, including Big Bass Bonanza Megaways, Big Bass Splash, Big Bass Day at the Races, and several seasonal variants. Each title maintains the core mechanic with variations in the reel structure, multiplier system, or fishing location theme.

Try the original: Big Bass Bonanza free play.

Buffalo King Megaways

Buffalo King Megaways is Pragmatic Play's most successful Megaways title and a strong performer in the South African market. The African savanna theme resonates locally, and the game delivers up to 200,704 ways to win on a 6x8 grid configuration. The free spins feature includes scatter pays, retriggers, and a straightforward free spins mechanic without a growing multiplier, which gives it a different rhythm from games like Gates of Olympus.

Pragmatic Play and Spina Zonke

Spina Zonke is the online slots platform integrated into Hollywoodbets, South Africa's largest sports betting operator. The platform hosts a curated selection of slots from multiple providers, with Pragmatic Play titles accounting for a significant proportion of the library.

The Spina Zonke name ("spin all" in isiZulu) reflects the platform's effort to connect with South African players in a culturally relevant way. The platform's integration with Hollywoodbets' existing sports betting interface introduced online slots to millions of South African players who already had established accounts and trusted the brand.

This distribution partnership was a major factor in Pragmatic Play's dominance of the South African slots market. Gates of Olympus and Sweet Bonanza became household names among South African online gamblers partly because they were prominently featured on Spina Zonke when the platform launched and gained popularity.
