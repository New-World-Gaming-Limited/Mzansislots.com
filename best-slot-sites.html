Bonus Transparency

Welcome bonuses can significantly boost your bankroll, but the fine print matters. The wagering requirement (sometimes called the "playthrough") tells you how many times you must bet the bonus amount before withdrawing. For example, a R1,000 bonus with a 30x wagering requirement means you need to place R30,000 worth of bets. Lower is better - anything under 40x is considered fair. Responsible Gambling in South Africa

Online gambling should always be enjoyable - never a source of financial stress. South Africa's gambling industry is overseen by the National Gambling Board and provincial boards like the Western Cape Gambling Board, which enforce strict rules to protect players.

Every licensed operator must provide responsible gambling tools, including deposit limits, loss limits, session time reminders, and self-exclusion options. If you feel that gambling is becoming a problem, these tools let you take a break or close your account entirely.

If you or someone you know needs help, contact the National Responsible Gambling Programme helpline at 0800 006 008 (toll-free, available 24/7). You can also visit responsiblegambling.co.za for counselling resources and self-assessment tools.
